Help & Tips

Getting Started:

1. Play and Pause: Click the play button to start playback and pause to stop.

2. Volume Control: Adjust the volume using your device's volume controls.

Navigation:

3. Browse Music: Explore your library by clicking on the tabs like "Playlists."

4. Search: Use the search bar to find specific songs, artists, or albums quickly.

Playback Controls:

5. Repeat: Enable repeat mode to play a single track or playlist on loop.

6. Shuffle: Click the shuffle button to play songs in random order.

Account and Settings:

7. User Account: Create an account to save playlists and preferences.

8. Settings: Customize your experience by adjusting playback settings, and more in the settings menu.

Troubleshooting:

9. No Sound: If you experience no sound, check your device's volume settings, and ensure your speakers or headphones are connected.

10. Browser Compatibility: Make sure you're using a supported browser for the best experience. We recommend the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge.

FAQs:

11. How do I create a playlist? - Visit the "Playlists" section and click "Create New Playlist."

Contact Support:

12. Need Help? - For further assistance or technical issues, contact our support team at melodymix@gmail.com.

Tips and Tricks:

13. Organize Your Library: Create playlists and categorize your music for easy access.

14. Discover New Music: Explore the "Suggested (home)" section to discover new tracks based on your listening history.
